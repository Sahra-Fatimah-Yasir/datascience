About MeWind as a Daily Wind Speed Forecasting App

Welcome to MeWind, a web-based forecasting system designed to provide accurate daily wind speed information for Tanjungpinang City. This system aims to support maritime activities, fisheries, and the mitigation of extreme weather risks in the region.

Technologies Used

  • Support Vector Machine (SVM): A robust algorithm for classification and regression, delivering reliable predictions by leveraging historical data patterns.
